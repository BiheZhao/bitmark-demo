Infinity, a state-of-the-art bitwise autoregressive model, builds an image across 13 progressive scales — from a 1×1 token to the final 64×64 grid. BitMark intervenes at the bit level, without slowing this process down.

№ 04 / 04 · THE TWIST
Radioactivity

The watermark spreads.

Train any new model on BitMark-watermarked images — even just fine-tune it — and the new model inherits the signature. Like a fingerprint passed down through generations.

Watch: one watermarked dataset infects every downstream model it touches.
