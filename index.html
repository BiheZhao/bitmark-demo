№ 03 / 04 · WHY IT MATTERS
Societal impact

Trust for the synthetic age.

Every month, billions of AI-generated images flood the internet. Without accountability, we lose the ability to tell real from synthetic — and models eventually collapse, training on their own outputs.

01
Pillar OneProvenance

Responsible disclosure of AI-generated content.

Model providers can transparently mark every image their systems produce. Journalists, platforms, and regulators gain a verifiable signal of synthetic origin — without compromising creative use.

Invisible to users · Unmistakable to detectors
02
Pillar TwoIntegrity

Countering misinformation and deepfakes.

From fabricated evidence to political impersonation, undetected synthetic media erodes public trust. A reliable, imperceptible signature is the foundation for content provenance at internet scale.

Robust to cropping, compression, filtering
№ 04 / 04 · THE TWIST
Radioactivity

The watermark spreads.

Train any new model on BitMark-watermarked images — even just fine-tune it — and the new model inherits the signature. Like a fingerprint passed down through generations.

Watch: one watermarked dataset infects every downstream model it touches.
